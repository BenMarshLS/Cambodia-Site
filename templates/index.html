Lincoln Sudbury Regional Memorial School



  No one ever expects a young person to die. Since its establishment in 1954, the Lincoln-Sudbury Regional High School community has unfortunately experienced a number of these deaths among its students and graduates. Out of these unimaginable tragedies comes this project of hope... The Lincoln-Sudbury Memorial School Project is an independent, student-driven effort that has and will continue to raise money to maintain a sister school in Cambodia, in memory of all former L-S students who died before their time. The project works with American Assistance for Cambodia, a not-for-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of rural Cambodian youth. We hope to promote the continued healing of the Lincoln-Sudbury community and to foster a sustained connection between schools, promoting positive social change in both communities.
